## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add a new post?

Drop a `.mdx` file in `content/blog` with the frontmatter shown in this sample,
add a hero image, and rebuild.

### How do topic hubs work?

Tags in frontmatter are mapped to the categories you define in
`lib/blog/config.ts`. Every post should map to at least one hub.

<Verdict>
Keep the visible FAQ section and the frontmatter `faq` array in sync — the
frontmatter feeds FAQPage JSON-LD, the visible section feeds readers.
</Verdict>
